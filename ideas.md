<text>
<idea>
  <design_movement>Cyberpunk Noir / High-Tech Industrial</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Raw Data Visualization**: UI elements should look like heads-up displays (HUDs) or terminal interfaces.
    2. **Neon Contrast**: Stark contrast between deep black backgrounds and vibrant neon accents (cyan, magenta, lime).
    3. **Glitch Aesthetics**: Subtle glitch effects on hover and transitions to emphasize the "AI" nature.
    4. **Modular Grid**: Strict, visible grid lines separating content areas, reminiscent of circuit boards.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Digital Underground"**: Reflects the cutting-edge, slightly rebellious nature of AI.
    - Background: #050505 (Void Black)
    - Primary: #00FF9D (Neon Mint)
    - Secondary: #FF0055 (Cyber Pink)
    - Text: #E0E0E0 (Light Gray)
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"Dashboard Command Center"**: Dense information density. Sidebar navigation with collapsible panels. Content is organized in "modules" rather than flowing sections.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Scanlines**: Subtle overlay texture on backgrounds.
    2. **Monospace Typography**: Use of coding fonts for headers and metadata.
    3. **Angled Edges**: Buttons and cards have 45-degree cut corners (chamfered).
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Tactile Digital"**: Hover effects trigger instant color inversions or "decoding" text animations. Clicks feel mechanical and responsive.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **"System Boot"**: Elements load with a typing effect or slide in from the edges with a snap. No soft fades; everything is sharp and calculated.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - Headers: 'JetBrains Mono' or 'Space Mono' (Technical, precise).
    - Body: 'Inter' (Legible, neutral).
  </typography_system>
</idea>
</text>

  <design_movement>Ethereal Glassmorphism / Deep Space</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Depth & Translucency**: Heavy use of backdrop-blur and semi-transparent layers to create a sense of floating in a 3D space.
    2. **Luminous Gradients**: Glowing light sources that illuminate content from behind, creating a "magical" feel.
    3. **Fluid Geometry**: Soft, rounded shapes and organic curves rather than sharp boxes.
    4. **Immersive Darkness**: A deep, rich background that isn't just black, but a gradient of deep blues and purples.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Nebula Drift"**: Evokes the mystery and vast potential of AI.
    - Background: #030712 (Rich Dark Blue/Black)
    - Primary: #8B5CF6 (Violet) -> #3B82F6 (Blue) gradients.
    - Accents: #F472B6 (Soft Pink) for highlights.
    - Text: #F8FAFC (Slate 50) for high readability against dark.
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"Floating Stream"**: Content cards float in the center, surrounded by ambient light. Less rigid grid, more breathing room.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Glass Cards**: `bg-white/5 backdrop-blur-md border-white/10`.
    2. **Orbital Glows**: CSS radial gradients positioned behind key elements.
    3. **Stardust Particles**: Subtle particle background effect.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Weightless Drift"**: Hovering over cards causes them to lift slightly and glow brighter. Smooth, damping transitions.
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **"Nebula Flow"**: Slow, pulsing gradients. Elements fade in and float upwards.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - Headers: 'Plus Jakarta Sans' (Geometric, modern, friendly).
    - Body: 'DM Sans' (Clean, highly readable).
  </typography_system>

<idea>
  <design_movement>Swiss International / Brutalist Minimal</design_movement>
  <core_principles>
    1. **Radical Simplicity**: stripping away all decoration. No shadows, no gradients, no blur.
    2. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold type is the primary visual element.
    3. **High Contrast**: Black and white dominant, with one striking accent color.
    4. **Grid Adherence**: Visible or strictly felt grid systems.
  </core_principles>
  <color_philosophy>
    **"Architectural Pure"**: Focus on content and structure.
    - Background: #FFFFFF (Paper White) or #F0F0F0 (Light Gray).
    - Primary: #000000 (Ink Black).
    - Accent: #FF3300 (International Orange).
  </color_philosophy>
  <layout_paradigm>
    **"Editorial Magazine"**: Asymmetric layouts, large whitespace, sticky headers.
  </layout_paradigm>
  <signature_elements>
    1. **Thick Borders**: 2px or 4px solid black borders on everything.
    2. **Marquee Text**: Scrolling text for announcements or trending items.
    3. **Oversized Icons**: Simple, geometric icons used at large scale.
  </signature_elements>
  <interaction_philosophy>
    **"Direct & Instant"**: No transition delays. Hover states are hard switches (e.g., white to black).
  </interaction_philosophy>
  <animation>
    **"Slide & Reveal"**: Content slides out from behind masks. Parallax scrolling for text.
  </animation>
  <typography_system>
    - Headers: 'Helvetica Now' or 'Archivo' (Bold, grotesque).
    - Body: 'Roboto Mono' (Technical contrast).
  </typography_system>
</idea>
</text>
